When we feel deeply connected and understood, we can live more authentically and joyfully. But when we feel stuck or conflicted, clarity can seem out of reach. With over 25 years of experience, I help clients work through challenges, heal from the past, and shift from simply doing to truly being -- clear, connected, and engaged in life. I specialize in supporting individuals and families who find themselves at a crossroads, whether in their relationships, careers, or ways of living. In my relationship work, I help partners or family members communicate more openly, understand each other more deeply, and create a stronger, more compassionate connection.

I offer both in-person sessions in my Wellesley office and online statewide.

If you’re struggling to find your way, figure out what’s next, heal from past experiences, or just feel better, please reach out to discuss how I can help.
